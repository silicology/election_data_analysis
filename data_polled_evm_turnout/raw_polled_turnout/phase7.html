PRESS NOTE

65.79% voter turnout recorded at polling stations in GE 2024

Voter turnout of 63.88 % recorded in phase-7 of General Elections 2024

In continuation of ECI two press notes dated 01.06.2024 and as per practice followed

for release of voter turnout figures in earlier phases, voter turnout of 63.88% has been

recorded at polling stations in phase-7 for 57 PCs in the General Elections 2024.

Overall, 65.79% voter turnout recorded in General Elections 2024 at the polling stations.

Detailed statistical reports having number of postal votes and Gross voter turnout will

be made available on the ECI website after finalisation of details, as received from

States/UTs in due course as per standard practice. Postal Ballots include postal ballots

given to service voters, absentee voters (85+, PwD, Essential Services etc.) and voters

on election duty.
